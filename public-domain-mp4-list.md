### 🍿 Feature Films

*   **Nosferatu (1922)** - [Link](https://dn710901.ca.archive.org/0/items/Nosferatu1922VHS/Nosferatu%20%281922%29.mp4)
    *   The foundational German Expressionist horror film featuring Count Orlok, the iconic and grotesque vampire.

*   **Nosferatu: A Symphony of Horror (1922 Alternate)** - [Link](https://dn711006.ca.archive.org/0/items/nosferatu_201508/dom-6567newnosferatu.mp4)
    *   An alternate cut of the 1922 classic, preserving the eerie atmosphere of the original silent masterpiece.

*   **Phantom of the Opera (1925)** - [Link](https://ia800201.us.archive.org/2/items/ThePhantomoftheOpera/Phantom_of_the_Opera_512kb.mp4)
    *   Lon Chaney stars as the deformed genius lurking beneath the Paris Opera House in this legendary silent horror.

*   **The General (1926)** - [Link](https://ia601301.us.archive.org/8/items/The_General_Buster_Keaton/The_General_512kb.mp4)
    *   Buster Keaton’s comedic action epic involving a high-speed train chase during the American Civil War.

*   **White Zombie (1932)** - [Link](https://ia803406.us.archive.org/11/items/white_zombie/white_zombie_512kb.mp4)
    *   Bela Lugosi stars in the first feature-length zombie film, set in Haiti and focusing on voodoo-driven undead.

*   **Reefer Madness (1936)** - [Link](https://ia601605.us.archive.org/11/items/reefer_madness1938/reefer_madness1938_512kb.mp4)
    *   A notorious propaganda film intended to warn parents about the "deadly" dangers of marijuana.

*   **House on Haunted Hill (1959)** - [Link](https://ia601306.us.archive.org/19/items/house_on_haunted_hill_ipod/house_on_haunted_hill_512kb.mp4)
    *   Vincent Price stars as an eccentric millionaire who offers five people money to survive a night in a haunted mansion.

*   **Last Woman on Earth (1960)** - [Link](https://dn721601.ca.archive.org/0/items/last.-woman.on.-earth.-1960.-/Last.Woman.on.Earth.1960.DVDRip.Xvid.BZ-CG.mp4)
    *   Three survivors of an apocalyptic event find themselves in a tense love triangle as they navigate a world without people.

*   **Little Shop of Horrors (1960)** - [Link](https://ia800102.us.archive.org/9/items/Little_ShopOf_Horrors.avi/The_Little_Shop_of_Horrors_512kb.mp4)
    *   A dark comedy about a florist’s assistant who raises a bloodthirsty plant that feeds on human flesh.

*   **Night of the Living Dead (1968)** - [Link](https://ia801409.us.archive.org/29/items/Night.Of.The.Living.Dead_1080p/NightOfTheLivingDead_iPod_512kb.mp4)
    *   George A. Romero’s genre-defining masterpiece where survivors barricade themselves in a farmhouse against reanimated corpses.

*   **Lost World (1925)** - [Link](https://ia800208.us.archive.org/15/items/lost_world/lost_world_512kb.mp4)
    *   A silent adventure epic featuring early stop-motion dinosaurs discovered on a remote plateau in South America.

*   **Killers from Space (1954)** - [Link](https://ia600704.us.archive.org/2/items/Killers_from_space/Killers_from_space_512kb.mp4)
    *   A nuclear scientist is resurrected by bug-eyed aliens who plan to use giant insects to conquer the Earth.

*   **The Brain That Wouldn't Die (1962)** - [Link](https://ia800305.us.archive.org/18/items/TheBrainThatWouldntDie/The_Brain_That_Wouldnt_Die_512kb.mp4)
    *   A scientist keeps his fiancée's severed head alive in a tray while searching for a new body for her.

*   **Carnival of Souls (1962)** - [Link](https://ia800300.us.archive.org/19/items/CarnivalofSouls/Carnival_of_Souls_512kb.mp4)
    *   Following a traumatic car accident, a woman is drawn to a mysterious, abandoned carnival and haunted by ghoulish figures.

### 📺 Educational & Shorts

*   **Hemp for Victory (1942)** - [Link](https://ia800200.us.archive.org/31/items/Hemp_for_victory_1942/Hemp_for_victory_1942_512kb.mp4)
    *   An educational film produced by the US government to encourage farmers to grow hemp for the WWII effort.

*   **Duck and Cover (1951)** - [Link](https://ia800203.us.archive.org/17/items/gov.ntis.AVA11048VNB1/AVA11048VNB1_512kb.mp4)
    *   A Cold War civil defense film featuring Bert the Turtle, teaching schoolchildren how to survive a nuclear blast.
